Clubs and Societies

Message from Chaplain

The school has got the chaplaincy program to help boost the ethos in support of the administration. Just to put it in a nut shell, chaplaincy provides pastoral care in a way which complements what is being provided by other members of staff. Chaplain could often be seen as a bridge to activate community building in a wider sense for the benefit of the school. Therefore chaplain does not have disciplinary responsibility but could listen to issues none judgmentally. The chaplain runs needs-based programs such as handling of grief or behavioral management. He also leads during the school events or special ceremonies and participates in school camps for the youths. Similarly the chaplains department provides guidance to students on issues concerning human relationships and by extension does off-site visits to parents and care givers. On Sunday’s all students pray together in the services irrespective of their religious background. The school provides spiritual aspect of every individual and factoring in God’s word in their programs.

The the school has three societies that help in spiritual nourishment of our students.These societes are: Christian Union(CU), Young singers(SDA), Young Christian Society (YCS), The chaplain and has greatly contributed to the success of these societies together with the support from their patrons.

Clubs

Debate Club

Debate was dated back to the 18th century having its origin from Greece. In mind 18th century debating had spread across the world and the greatest part of it was when it was introduced to Meteitei Boys High school. Under the leadership of Mr. Samson Bet as the club Patron and Evans Kibungei as the chairperson the club has been able to attend the great debaters contest competitions and attainted position 4 in the region. We look forward to this year’s completions aiming the National trophy .Our aim as a club is to build an act of public speaking by bulging confidence and courage in every debater in order to speak confidently and express ideas that will enable him as an individual, as the school and the society at large to grow. We aim to nature in born abilities in various students and keep them in line with the vibrant world of tomorrow.!

Sports

Sports Patron

The role of sports in school cannot be underscored. It goes with to adage; all work with no play makes Jack a dull boy. As such Meteitei boys offers magnificent opportunities for sporting activities .This is tandem with the national goal of education. The school offers volleyball, football, handball, table tennis, rugby, hockey athletics, basketball and badminton and law tennis. The school has begun the journey in reclaiming its glory as a sport powerhouse. We have proved indomitable over the years and the years 2016 was great considering that our hockey team played final at the county despite of the challenge of lack of playing field for practice .Badminton also did us proud by winning the county trophy and representing Nandi county in the regional completion. With our unweaving faith in our abilities we hope to reach National completions. Acknowledging the giants of the valley the school administration offers the teams and their coaches utmost support such us, facilitating tournament, training of couches and supply of games equipments.
